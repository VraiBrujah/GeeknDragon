After a fight, you can recover half the bolts used by spending 1 minute searching the area.\n\n>- **Loading**: You can only fire one bolt per **Action**, **Bonus Action**, or **Reaction**, regardless of the number of attacks you can normally make.\n\n>- **Two-Handed**: Requires two hands to use effectively, leaving no room for shields or additional weapons.\n\n---\n\n**Mastery**\n\n**Slow** – If you hit a creature with this weapon and deal damage, you can reduce its speed by **10ft (3 m, 2sq)** until the start of your next turn. This effect does not stack with additional Slow effects.
