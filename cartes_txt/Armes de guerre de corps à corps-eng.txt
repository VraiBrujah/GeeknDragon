If proficient, add your **Proficiency Bonus** to the attack roll.\n\n---\n\n**Properties**:\n\n>- **Thrown**: You can throw the trident to make a ranged attack. Its range is **20ft (6 m, 4sq)** for normal range and **60ft (18 m, 12sq)** for long range. Attacking beyond the normal range imposes disadvantage on the attack roll.\n\n>- **Versatile**: When wielded with two hands, the trident deals <br>**1d10 piercing damage** instead of 1d8.\n\n---\n\n**Mastery**\n\n**Topple** – If you hit a creature with this weapon, you can force it to make a **Constitution saving throw**. The DC for this save is calculated as 8 + your **Proficiency Bonus** + your **Strength modifier**. On a failed save, the creature is knocked **Prone**, giving melee attackers advantage on their next attacks against it and imposing disadvantage on ranged attacks targeting it.
